Our Core Objectives

We are committed to improving the health, wellbeing, protection, and empowerment of mothers, children, and vulnerable communities through sustainable interventions and strategic partnerships.

Health & HIV Prevention

  • Promote safe pregnancy practices to reduce mother-to-child HIV transmission.
  • Enhance access to antiretroviral therapy for HIV-positive mothers.
  • Raise awareness on regular HIV testing and counseling for pregnant women.
  • Promote breastfeeding practices that minimize HIV transmission risk.
  • Programs supporting the global HIV target of 95–95–95.

Child Wellbeing & Protection

  • Improve children’s overall health and wellbeing.
  • Provide vulnerable children (MVCs) with access to healthcare and education.
  • Promote safety by preventing mistreatment, exploitation, and harmful behaviors.
  • Connect children to nutrition, vaccination, and medical treatment campaigns.
  • Encourage early detection and prevention of illnesses.

Education & Awareness

  • Raise awareness on good nutrition and hygiene.
  • Promote health education for parents, caregivers, and children.
  • Empower communities with knowledge to support HIV-positive mothers and children.
  • Conduct research, observation, and learning assessments to improve programs.

Community Empowerment

  • Educate and involve caregivers and MVCs in income-generating activities.
  • Equip communities with resources to support maternal and child health.
  • Build cooperation with the Tanzanian government and partner organizations.
  • Strengthen community resilience through long-term support frameworks.
